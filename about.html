about the company

How it all started

Magic Studios started in a garage. Once a week, we would gather our camera tools and go out into the world capturing pictures of one another. Posting them on social media we gained popularity on the diverse colours, effects and personalities we had.

What we did about it

One group of friends, turned into a company with so much love and
spirit. Based in Ottawa, Ontario, we travel around Canada making sure
the world experiences the memories we had, just like that summer in the
garage.
